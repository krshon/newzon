Our Story

We believe in a transparent world where news is verified, trustworthy, and free of bias.

In today’s fast-paced world, where information is consumed rapidly, distinguishing between truth and misinformation has never been more challenging. Our mission began with a simple yet powerful question: Why should people have to guess if the news they read is real?

Why We Started

News can be overwhelming—but it often lacks transparency. We saw the gap: a platform that shows not just facts but also their reliability and political bias. That's when NEWZON was born.

Our Mission

Our goal is to deliver verified news with complete transparency. With tools like fact-checking, a bias indicator, and a truth index, readers can trust what they consume.

How We Work: Truth Index & Political Bias Bar

Truth Index

The Truth Index is our unique metric for assessing news reliability. It uses verified sources, fact-checking databases, and AI to detect manipulation. A score closer to 100% means the news is highly reliable.

Truth Score: 92% — Verified by trusted sources and minimal opinionated content.

Political Bias

The Political Bias Bar helps visualize where a source stands politically. It analyzes tone, sources, references, and history. Categories include Left, Right, and Neutral.

Bias: 30% Left, 20% Neutral, 50% Right
